Problem Statement

Small farmers struggle to reach city markets and depend on middlemen, which lowers their income. Urban consumers pay higher prices and get poor-quality, less-fresh produce. A broken supply chain leads to waste and unsustainable practices.

Our Solution

  • Digital platform connects farmers directly with urban consumers and businesses
  • Logistics and fair-pricing mechanisms with direct delivery—no middlemen
  • Features: real-time listings, traceability, efficient transport, secure payments, transparent ratings
